Tomato Egg Stir Fry

Tomato Egg is a savory, sweet, and sour dish from China that has been growing to be a well known egg dish around the world. This dish is perfect for many occasions, from having a bad day, or feeling a little sick. This nostalgic dish will punch those feelings away and allow you to feel "in the moment" with how creamy and fluffy each bite is.

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • green onion for garnish
  • sea salt and pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water. While the water is boiling, take out ingredients and wash the tomato.
  2. Use a knife to make a "plus sign (+)" incision in the tomato (just enough to pierce the skin) on the opposite end of the stem.
  3. When the water is boiling, place the tomato in for around 30-60 seconds.
  4. During this time, take out a bowl and fill it up with ice and water.
  5. After the time has passed, safely drain and take the tomato out and put it into the "ice bath."
  6. Heat the pot over medium heat until it is dry.
  7. During this time, take the tomato from the ice bath and begin peeling the skin off. After the skin is peeled, cut the tomato into cubes of desired size and set aside.
  8. In the dry, heated pot, drizzle olive oil and crack the eggs in.
  9. Move the eggs around and "scramble" them until they are just under cooked, then set aside. Use appropriate cookware.
  10. Use more olive oil as needed, and begin to cook the cubed tomatoes over medium heat. Cook until lightly tender (around 1-2 minutes)
  11. Add the eggs back into the pot, add the ketchup and soy sauce
  12. Let it simmer, stir occasionally
  13. Salt and Pepper to taste, give it one last stir
  14. Serve on a bed of rice and garnish with green onion
  15. Enjoy!
