Kalbi

Galbi (also known as Kalbi) is a staple in korean cuisine when it comes to their famous Korean BBQ. Kalbi is a marinated sweet and savory short ribs dish cooked right in front of you. During the time it takes to cook, the exchange of words between the guests as they take in a puff of the delighted aromas that emit from this cut of beef; there really is no better description as to how holy this experience may be for people.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lb korean beef short ribs
  • 1 asain pear, peeled, cored, minced
  • medium sweet onion (yellow onion is fine as well); chopped and minced
  • 1 piece fresh ginger, peeled and minced
  • 7 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped for garnish
